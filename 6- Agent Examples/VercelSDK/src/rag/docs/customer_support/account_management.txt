## Customer Support - Account Management

### Password Reset Process

To reset a password, customers should:
1. Navigate to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password" link
3. Enter their registered email address
4. Check email for reset link (valid for 24 hours)
5. Click link and create new password (minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)

If customer doesn't receive email:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Wait 5-10 minutes for delivery
- Contact support if still not received after 15 minutes

### Account Verification

New accounts require email verification:
- Verification email sent immediately upon registration
- Account is locked until email is verified
- Verification link expires after 48 hours
- Can request new verification email from account settings

### Subscription Management

Customers can manage subscriptions from their account dashboard:
- View current plan and billing cycle
- Upgrade or downgrade plans
- Cancel subscription (access continues until end of billing period)
- Update payment method
- View billing history

Cancellation policy:
- Cancellations take effect at end of current billing period
- No refunds for partial months
- Data retained for 30 days after cancellation
- Can reactivate within 30 days without losing data

### Account Security

Security best practices:
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for enhanced security
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Never share account credentials
- Log out from shared devices
- Review account activity regularly

If account is compromised:
- Immediately change password
- Enable 2FA if not already active
- Review recent account activity
- Contact support to report suspicious activity
- Support can lock account if needed

### Profile Updates

Customers can update:
- Email address (requires verification)
- Display name
- Profile picture
- Notification preferences
- Privacy settings

Email changes require:
- Current password confirmation
- Verification of new email address
- 7-day grace period where old email still works
