## Customer Support - Billing and Refunds

### Payment Methods

Accepted payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (for enterprise accounts)
- Wire transfers (for annual enterprise plans)

Payment processing:
- All payments processed securely through Stripe
- Cards are charged automatically on billing date
- Failed payments result in 3 retry attempts over 7 days
- Account access suspended after payment failure

### Billing Cycles

Available billing cycles:
- Monthly: Charged on same date each month
- Annual: Charged once per year, 2 months free (save 16%)
- Enterprise: Custom billing terms available

Billing date:
- Set to date of first subscription
- Can be changed by contacting support
- Prorated charges apply for mid-cycle changes

### Refund Policy

Refund eligibility:
- Full refunds available within 30 days of initial purchase
- Partial refunds for annual plans prorated based on unused time
- No refunds for monthly subscriptions after first month
- Enterprise contracts follow terms in service agreement

Refund process:
1. Customer submits refund request through support portal
2. Support reviews request (typically within 24 hours)
3. If approved, refund processed within 5-7 business days
4. Refund appears on original payment method
5. Account access revoked upon refund completion

Refund exceptions:
- No refunds for accounts terminated for terms of service violations
- Custom enterprise contracts may have different terms
- Refunds not available for add-on services purchased separately

### Invoice Management

Customers can access invoices:
- Download from account dashboard
- Email copies sent automatically each billing cycle
- Historical invoices available for 7 years
- Export invoices for accounting purposes

Invoice information includes:
- Service period covered
- Itemized charges
- Tax information (where applicable)
- Payment method used
- Invoice number for records

### Billing Disputes

To dispute a charge:
1. Contact support within 60 days of charge
2. Provide invoice number and reason for dispute
3. Support investigates (typically 3-5 business days)
4. Resolution communicated via email
5. If dispute valid, refund or credit issued

Common billing issues:
- Duplicate charges: Usually resolved within 24 hours
- Incorrect amounts: Verified against service agreement
- Unauthorized charges: Account security review initiated
- Failed payment fees: May be waived for first occurrence
