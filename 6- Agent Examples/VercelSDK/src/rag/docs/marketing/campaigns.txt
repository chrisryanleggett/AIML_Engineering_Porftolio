## Marketing - Campaign Management

### Campaign Types

Product Launch Campaigns:
- Pre-launch: Teaser content, waitlist signups
- Launch day: Announcement across all channels
- Post-launch: Follow-up content, user testimonials
- Duration: 4-6 weeks total

Awareness Campaigns:
- Goal: Increase brand recognition
- Channels: Social media, content marketing, PR
- Target: Broad audience, potential customers
- Metrics: Reach, impressions, brand mentions

Acquisition Campaigns:
- Goal: Drive new signups and conversions
- Channels: Paid ads, email, content marketing
- Target: Qualified prospects
- Metrics: Cost per acquisition, conversion rate

Retention Campaigns:
- Goal: Keep existing customers engaged
- Channels: Email, in-app messaging, content
- Target: Existing users
- Metrics: Retention rate, engagement, upsells

### Campaign Planning

Campaign planning process:
1. Define objectives: What do we want to achieve?
2. Identify target audience: Who are we reaching?
3. Choose channels: Where will we reach them?
4. Create content: What will we say?
5. Set budget: How much will we spend?
6. Define timeline: When will this run?
7. Establish metrics: How will we measure success?

Campaign brief template:
- Campaign name and objective
- Target audience persona
- Key message and value proposition
- Channels and tactics
- Budget allocation
- Timeline and milestones
- Success metrics and KPIs

### Paid Advertising

Advertising platforms:
- Google Ads: Search and display advertising
- Facebook/Instagram Ads: Social media advertising
- LinkedIn Ads: B2B professional targeting
- Twitter Ads: Real-time engagement
- Programmatic: Automated ad buying

Ad campaign structure:
- Campaign: High-level objective
- Ad Groups: Grouped by theme or audience
- Keywords/Targeting: Who sees the ads
- Ads: Creative variations
- Landing Pages: Where users land

Budget allocation:
- 40% on proven performers
- 30% on testing new channels
- 20% on retargeting
- 10% on experimental tactics

### Email Marketing Campaigns

Email campaign types:
- Welcome series: Onboarding new users
- Newsletter: Regular content updates
- Product updates: Feature announcements
- Promotional: Special offers and discounts
- Re-engagement: Win-back inactive users

Email best practices:
- Subject lines: Clear, compelling, < 50 characters
- Personalization: Use recipient name and preferences
- Mobile optimization: Responsive design
- Clear CTA: Single, prominent call-to-action
- A/B testing: Test subject lines and content

Email metrics:
- Open rate: Target > 25%
- Click-through rate: Target > 3%
- Conversion rate: Target > 2%
- Unsubscribe rate: Target < 0.5%
- Bounce rate: Target < 2%

### Social Media Campaigns

Platform strategies:
- LinkedIn: B2B content, thought leadership
- Twitter: Real-time engagement, quick updates
- Facebook: Community building, user stories
- Instagram: Visual content, behind-the-scenes
- YouTube: Video tutorials and demos

Content mix:
- 40% educational content
- 30% product updates
- 20% user-generated content
- 10% promotional content

Engagement tactics:
- Respond to comments within 24 hours
- Ask questions to encourage interaction
- Share user content and testimonials
- Run contests and giveaways
- Use relevant hashtags

### Campaign Analytics

Campaign tracking:
- UTM parameters: Track traffic sources
- Conversion tracking: Measure goal completions
- Attribution modeling: Understand customer journey
- ROI calculation: Revenue vs. campaign cost

Reporting dashboard includes:
- Campaign performance overview
- Channel comparison
- Cost per acquisition by channel
- Conversion funnel analysis
- Revenue attribution
- Trend analysis over time

Key performance indicators:
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Target > 3:1
- Cost per Acquisition (CPA): Track by channel
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV): Measure long-term value
- Campaign ROI: Revenue / Campaign Cost
- Engagement rate: Interactions / Impressions
