## Marketing - Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

Our content strategy focuses on four main pillars:
1. Educational: How-to guides, tutorials, best practices
2. Thought Leadership: Industry insights, trends, analysis
3. Product Updates: Feature announcements, release notes
4. Community: User stories, case studies, testimonials

Content goals:
- Establish brand as industry authority
- Drive organic traffic through SEO
- Educate potential customers
- Engage existing user community
- Support sales and customer success teams

### Content Types

Blog posts:
- How-to guides: Step-by-step tutorials
- Industry analysis: Trends and insights
- Case studies: Customer success stories
- Product updates: New features and improvements
- Best practices: Tips and recommendations

Social media:
- LinkedIn: Professional content, industry news
- Twitter: Quick tips, product updates, engagement
- Facebook: Community building, user stories
- Instagram: Visual content, behind-the-scenes

Email marketing:
- Newsletter: Monthly roundup of content and updates
- Product updates: Feature announcements
- Educational series: Multi-part tutorials
- Re-engagement: Win-back campaigns

### SEO Strategy

SEO objectives:
- Rank in top 3 for 20+ target keywords
- Increase organic traffic by 50% year-over-year
- Build domain authority through quality backlinks
- Improve technical SEO scores

Keyword strategy:
- Primary keywords: High-volume, high-intent terms
- Long-tail keywords: Specific, lower competition
- Topic clusters: Comprehensive coverage of topics
- Local SEO: Geographic targeting where applicable

Content optimization:
- Target keyword in title and first paragraph
- Use H2/H3 headings with related keywords
- Include internal and external links
- Optimize images with alt text
- Meta descriptions: 150-160 characters, compelling

### Content Calendar

Content publishing schedule:
- Blog: 2-3 posts per week
- Social media: Daily posts across platforms
- Email newsletter: Monthly
- Video content: Weekly
- Webinars: Monthly

Content planning process:
1. Monthly content planning meeting
2. Assign topics to writers
3. Create editorial calendar
4. Review and approve content
5. Schedule publication dates
6. Promote across channels

### Content Performance Metrics

Key metrics to track:
- Page views: Total and unique visitors
- Time on page: Engagement indicator
- Bounce rate: Content relevance
- Social shares: Content virality
- Email open rates: Subject line effectiveness
- Conversion rate: Content driving actions

Performance targets:
- Blog post views: > 1000 views in first month
- Average time on page: > 2 minutes
- Bounce rate: < 60%
- Social engagement rate: > 3%
- Email open rate: > 25%
- Content conversion rate: > 2%

### Brand Voice and Tone

Brand voice characteristics:
- Professional yet approachable
- Clear and concise
- Helpful and educational
- Authentic and trustworthy
- Innovative and forward-thinking

Tone variations:
- Blog posts: Informative, friendly
- Social media: Conversational, engaging
- Documentation: Clear, precise
- Marketing materials: Compelling, benefit-focused
- Support content: Empathetic, solution-oriented
